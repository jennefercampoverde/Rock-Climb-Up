Welcome to Rock Climb Up

Whether you're a seasoned climber or new to the sport, our gym is designed to inspire and challenge climbers of all skill levels. At Rock Climb Up, you will find a vibrant community, state-of-the-art climbing walls, and a variety of routes tailored to beginners and experts alike.

Our Services

We offer all sorts of class ranging in difficulty from Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced as well as events for everyone!

Class Description

Some classes we are offer are:

  • Introduction to Climbing
  • Intermediate Climbing
  • Advanced Climbing
  • Belaying
  • Introduction to Bouldering
  • Advanced Bouldering
  • Private Climbing Classes

And if you don't see a class here you are interested in or if you have an instructor that can offer a class that is not already on our list, feel free to contact us with any questions and suggestions!
